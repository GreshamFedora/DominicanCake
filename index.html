About us and Dominican cake

We are diligent crafters of cake that strive to bake the tastiest cakes around in the traditional Dominican style. Dominican cakes, for those that haven't already had the pleasure, are a style of cake that have attracted fans no matter their ethnic or national background. Often found in traditional Dominican enclaves such as New York City where Dominican cakes are a popular choice for birthday parties and all manner of occasions. Dominican cake, or bizcocho dominicano, made its way to the United States from the Dominican Republic thanks to immigrants seeking to carry on the tradition that yields tasty, fluffy cakes with a merengue frosting and a thin sweet layer, such as of pineapple or guava filling, found in the middle.
